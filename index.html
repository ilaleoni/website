About Me

I'm a PhD student in Finance at Bocconi University and an Analyst on the Credit Derivatives Trading Desk at Intesa Sanpaolo. I hold a BSc in Mathematical Engineering from Politecnico di Milano and an MSc in Mathematical Modelling – with a focus on Machine Learning and Signal Processing – from the Technical University of Denmark. My research interests include Asset Pricing, Financial Technology, Applied Mathematics, and Blockchain applications in FinTech. I'm particularly interested in how advanced quantitative methods and AI can be applied to financial markets and real-world decision-making. I'm always open to collaborations, new ideas, and constructive feedback.
